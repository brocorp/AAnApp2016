Ticket Information

Coronation Tickets will go on sale Monday, October 17 at 9:00 a.m. at the Arkalalah Office. Reserved seats will be $8.00.

Carnival Tickets for the Wednesday night, "All You Can Ride Night" and the Friday 1:00 - 5:00 p.m. "All You Can Ride Afternoon," will be available at the Arkalalah office, Community National Bank, CornerBank, Union State Bank, and RCB Bank. Tickets must be purchased at the Ark City locations before noon on Wednesday, October 26. After that time, tickets may be purchased at the carnival for $18.00.

Any changes because of bad weather will be made at the discretion of the carnival and announced over local radio stations.
