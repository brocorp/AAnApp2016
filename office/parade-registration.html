Parade Registration

It's easy to be in any of the Arkalalah parades. Entry forms for the Wednesday night "Westar Parade of Lights" and for the Big Saturday Parade may be picked up at the Arkalalah office or printed from arkalalah.com and then faxed or brought in to the Arkalalah Office. Registration must be turned in before 5:00 p.m. on Friday, October 21. No cost to enter.

No entry is required for the Kiddie Parade.
