Two Mile Run Registration

Registration for the Ark City Rec Center Two Mile Run may be made at the Rec Center, 225 East Fifth Street, Arkansas City. Register the day of the race from 7:30 a.m. until 8:30 a.m. at the Arkalalah office. Cost to register before October 27 is $10 for those under 18 years of age and $15 for those over 18. Fees after October 27 are $15 for those under 18 and $20 for everyone else.

Early registration is due by October 27 and will guarantee an entrant a T-Shirt. Registrants after October 27 will receive shirts only while supplies last.

For more information, contact the Ark City Rec Center at (620) 441-4300. Registration form at arkcity.org/RecCenter and arkalalah.com.
