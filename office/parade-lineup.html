Festival Information
Parade Line-Up

The "Parade of Lights" on Wednesday, October 26, begins on the corner of Summit and Birch. All parade entries will line up on Summit as directed by the Arkalalah Committee. Please do not line up on the side streets.

The "Kiddie Parade" starts at 3:00 p.m. on Friday, October 28, at the corner of Summit and Chestnut. Judging for prizes will begin at 2:30. No motorized entries will be allowed.

The "Big Parade" begins at Wilson Park at 2:00 p.m. on Saturday, October 29. The parade ends at Adams Street. The parade line-up will be posted by Thursday, October 27 in the window of the Chamber of Commerce, published in the Cowley CourierTraveler as space allows, and at arkalalah.com.

Parade participants are asked not to call the Arkalalah Office for parade line-up information. Please check other resources first.

For the Saturday parade, floats will line-up on Summit Street north of Pine. Floats must enter from Kansas Avenue. There wil be no entry from side streets. Parade participants should walk to Wilson Park and be directed to their location by Arkalalah personnel. Exact float locations will not be available until parade line-up on Saturday.
