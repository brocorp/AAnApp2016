ACHS Class of 1966 Class Reunion

Friday, October 28 and Saturday, October 29

Class of 1966 will be holding their 50th class reunion on Friday, October 28th and Saturday, October 29th at First Council Hotel. Friday night is free for classmate and one guest. Saturday night is open with registration. Please call 620-366-1115. The cost is $25 per person and includes dinner and a live band. The festivities start at 6:30 pm on Saturday.
